About DivciPrince

A visionary leader with a track record of excellence, DivciPrince has been at the forefront of student advocacy, championing causes that matter. From organizing groundbreaking events to leading impactful initiatives, his journey is nothing short of inspiring.

With a heart for service and a mind for strategy, DivciPrince has held prestigious positions within the student union, including Public Relations Officer SUG 22/23 and Class representative Computer science department 2019 set till date. His leadership style is inclusive, empowering every voice to be heard and every idea to flourish.

Manifesto

My name is Divine Prince Daniel, and I am running for the office of Student Union President. My campaign, SURGE WITH DIVCIPRINCE, is focused on building a brighter future for all students. My experience and dedication to student welfare, academic excellence, and transparent governance form the core of my manifesto.

Key Goals:

  • Enhancing Student Welfare: Improve campus facilities and amenities. Increase support services for mental health and well-being. Advocate for affordable and quality healthcare services.
  • Promoting Academic Excellence: Provide resources and support for academic success. Organize skill development programs and workshops. Facilitate access to internships and job placements.
  • Ensuring Transparent Governance: Implement transparent financial management and reporting. Foster open communication between the union and students. Encourage student participation in decision-making processes.

Agenda

The key goals include enhancing student welfare, promoting academic excellence, and ensuring transparent governance.

- S: Strengthen - Strengthen the student community through unity and collective action.

- U : Uplift - Uplift student welfare by addressing key issues and providing necessary resources.

- R: Represent - Represent all student voices fairly and transparently in decision-making processes.

- G: Grow - Grow opportunities for academic and personal development through various programs and initiatives.

- E: Empower - Empower students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed and make a positive impact.

Goals

  • Support: Foster a supportive environment where every student feels valued and heard.
  • Unify: Build a united student body through inclusive events and initiatives.
  • Revitalize: Revamp campus life with innovative programs and engaging activities.
  • Guide: Provide guidance and resources for academic and career success.
  • Engage: Enhance student engagement through transparent governance and active participation in decision-making.
